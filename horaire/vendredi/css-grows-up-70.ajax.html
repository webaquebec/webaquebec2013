CSS Grows Up

vendredi le 22 février, de 15 h 00 à 16 h 30 Grand Hall

A look at how CSS development has evolved—much like JavaScript and server-side languages. From modular architecture to preprocessors and other considerations, we'll see how many teams are managing complex projects.

Biographie

Jonathan writes about tips, tricks, and bookmarks on his blog at Snook.ca. He has also written for A List Apart, 24ways, and .net magazine, and has co-authored two books, The Art and Science of CSS and Accelerated DOM Scripting. He has also authored and received world-wide acclaim for the self-published book, Scalable and Modular Architecture for CSS, sharing his experience and best practices on CSS architecture. When not writing books and speaking at conferences, Snook works on the design team at Shopify.
